Who we are?

FreeTorunGuide is an organization created to help foreign tourists arrange their free time in Torun, Poland.

Discover Torun!

We offer profesional tours led by students ready to show you the town and tell you a lot about UNESCO heritage site of Old Town, best bars and clubs, alternative art in town or history of Poland.

Free for real!

All tours are absouletly free of charge. If you enjoy our trip you can tip a guide.

Out trips. Choose the best for you.

If you would like a guided tour, send us an email with your name, nationality, preferred date and hour to torunfreeguide@gmail.com. You will be replied the next day. Note that we do not guide school trips. All tours start from the Old Town, meeting point is Cosmopolis fountain (see map below). Basic tour around the old town is approximetly 2 hours long.
We also offer special tours about Polish streetart, food and beverages or communism in Poland. To sign on for one send an email to torunfreeguide@gmail.com with annotation "Special trip".

About us

Tours are held by high school students, all with Cambridge FCE and CAE certificates. Money collected from tips will be donated for Model United Nations meeting ToMun2015. Free tours are present in every major European city, so we have thought tourists in our hometown deserve such services too.
